<CodeRunner code={`
zztool.setCookie('name', 'zzcpt',{
  expires: 1000*60*60 // 一小时后过期
})
console.log(zztool.getCookie('name'))
zztool.removeCookie('name')
console.log(zztool.getCookie('name')) // null

`} />

### props
<br/>
#### setCookie
| 属性名 | 说明 | 类型 | 必填 | 默认值 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| *name* | *名称* | *String* | *✅* | *-* |
| *value* | *索引* | *\** | *✅* | *-* |
| *options* | *配置项* | *Object* | *❌* | *-* |
| *options.expires* | *过期时间* | *Number* | *❌* | *-* |
| *options.path* | *路径* | *String* | *❌* | *-* |
| *options.domain* | *域名* | *String* | *❌* | *-* |
| *options.secure* | *安全性* | *String* | *❌* | *-* |
| *options.sameSite* | *安全性* | *Strict \| Lax \| None* | *❌* | *-* |
#### getCookie
| 属性名 | 说明 | 类型 | 必填 | 默认值 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| *name* | *名称* | *String* | *✅* | *-* |
#### removeCookie
| 属性名 | 说明 | 类型 | 必填 | 默认值 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| *name* | *名称* | *String* | *✅* | *-* |
| *path* | *路径* | *String* | *❌* | *-* |
