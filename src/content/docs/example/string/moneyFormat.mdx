const data2 = zztool.moneyFormat(123456789, "~", "￥") // ￥123~456~789
console.log(data1, data2)
`} />

### props
| 属性名 | 说明 | 类型 | 必填 | 默认值 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| *money* | *金额* | *String \| Number* | *✅* | *-* |
| *char* | *分隔符* | *Object* | *❌* | *","* |
| *first* | *首字符* | *String* | *❌* | *空字符* |
{/* | *format* | *格式* | *String* | *❌* | *hh:mm* | */}
